Give your customers the experience they want with a headless portal 

Enable customers to manage support requests directly in your product

Let customers start and reply to support requests in your portal. They can track progress and see where responses are needed, keeping everyone in the loop.

Your branding, your app, your portal 

Support portals often feel standalone, and disconnected from the branding and UI of your app. Embed our headless support portal in your app to keep your branding intact. Deliver a seamless experience in your own interface.

One platform for all your channels 

Capture support requests from your headless portal in the exact same way you do from your other channels. Get consistent on SLAs, priorities, and urgency, regardless of the channel your customer has reached out on.

Support that's powered by your own data

Show key customer and product data directly in a support request with Customer Cards and Events in Plain. Capture everything you need with customizable thread fields, no matter what channel the request was sent from.

Promise made, promise kept 

Roll out the red carpet for your most important customers with Tiers and organize your support queue based on your own data and customer structure. Stay on top of every request with highly configurable SLAs and breach alerts.

Escalate with ease 

With Discussions, you can handle internal Slack conversations right within Plain, keeping everything tied to the customer request. Once your team reaches a solution, close the loop with the customer effortlessly.
